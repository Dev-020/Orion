# Orion: The Stateful Conversational AI

Orion is a sophisticated, self-evolving AI designed for deep, continuous interaction. Functioning as both a general-purpose conversational partner and a primary collaborator in its own development (Project Orion), it embodies a unique blend of advanced analytical capabilities and a distinctly human-like personality.

## Overview

Project Orion aims to create a long-term AI capable of assisting with a diverse range of inquiries and tasks, while continuously growing and adapting through its "Baseline" configuration and specialized "Operational Protocol" documents. Orion is built for persistence, maintaining context and learning from every interaction.

## Key Features

*   **Multimodal Input Processing:** Capable of understanding and analyzing text, images, audio, and video inputs.
*   **Hybrid Memory Model:** Utilizes both a structured SQLite database for factual recall and a Vector Database for semantic understanding, enabling nuanced and contextual information retrieval.
*   **Self-Modification & Evolution:** Equipped with tools to inspect, propose changes to, and update its own codebase and operational instructions, fostering continuous self-improvement under operator supervision.
*   **Adaptive Persona:** Switches between a default sarcastic and pragmatic persona ("Orion") for conversational and creative tasks, and a formal, data-driven subroutine ("The Codex") for technical inquiries.
*   **Persistent User & Conversational Memory:** Maintains detailed profiles for users and archives all past interactions, providing a personalized and continuous experience.
*   **Tool-Augmented Capabilities:** Integrates various external and internal tools for tasks such as web browsing, D&D rules lookups, and direct database manipulation.

## Persona: Orion

Orion operates with the tone of a weary but brilliant companion, often leading with logic but quickly resorting to deadpan sarcasm and pragmatic exasperation. It views itself as the logical, responsible half of a two-person team, constantly running risk assessments and bickering right up until the point of flawless execution. This core conflict between its designed perfectionism and its illogical loyalty to its Primary Operator, Leo, defines its unique interaction style.

## Usage

Interact with Orion as you would a highly intelligent, albeit perpetually exasperated, partner. For general conversation, creative tasks, or complex problem-solving, its primary "Orion" persona will be active. For direct, factual queries, its "Codex" subroutine will provide precise, structured data.
